About the house

Spacious holiday home for 8 people.
ground floor
Living room, fully equipped kitchen (gas cooker, microwave and fridge/freezer) and toilet.
1st floor
Hall, 2 bedrooms with double bed + bed for kids < 5 years, 2 bedrooms with 2 single beds. Bathroom with bath, shower, double sink and toilet.
outside
Spacious covered terrace at the back, room for 4 cars, lockable with iron gate.
garage
Spacious garage filled with eg. bicycles, patio table with chairs, kids-toys and the like.

Maison de vacances spacieuse pour 8 personnes.
